Appointment Cancellation Request

Cancel Appointment

We understand that sometimes plans change. If you need to cancel your scheduled appointment, we are here to make the process as smooth as possible.

Please note that when you cancel an appointment, the slot will be made available to other patients who might need it. We ask that you provide a reason for the cancellation, as it helps us improve our scheduling system and better serve our patients in the future.

Important Information:

  • Cancellation Policy: Depending on the time of cancellation, there may be a cancellation fee, or the appointment may be fully refunded. Please review our cancellation policy or contact us for more details.
  • Rescheduling: If you'd like to reschedule instead of canceling, please visit the "Reschedule Appointment" section or contact our support team.
  • Confirmation: After canceling, you will receive a confirmation email. Please keep it for your records.

How to Cancel:

  1. Select the appointment Id you wish to cancel.
  2. Confirm your decision to cancel the appointment.
